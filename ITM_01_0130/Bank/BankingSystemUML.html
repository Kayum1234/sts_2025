UML Legend:

• Composition: Bank contains Customers, Accounts, Managers, and ATMs
• Association: Customer has many Accounts, Account belongs to one Customer
• Aggregation: Bank aggregates Managers and ATMs
• Dependencies: ATM uses Account for transactions
• Multiplicity: 1 Bank has 0..* Customers, 1 Customer has 1..* Accounts

System Architecture Overview:

• Bank: Central system managing all entities
• Customer: Bank clients with personal information
• Account: Financial accounts linked to customers
• Manager: Administrative users with permissions
• ATM: Automated teller machines for transactions
• VerificationSystem: Security and authentication layer
